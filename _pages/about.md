I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Mathematics, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), since December 2025. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Southeast University in 2025, my M.S. in Statistics from Southeast University in 2021, and my B.S. in Statistics from Yangzhou University in 2018.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on stochastic control theory and its applications in mathematical finance, with particular interests in:

- Linear-quadratic optimal control and stochastic differential games
- Mean-field stochastic systems and regime-switching diffusions
- Hawkes (self-exciting) jump-diffusion models
- Algebraic Riccati equations and their numerical methods
